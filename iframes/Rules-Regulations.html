1/7

Traffic Light Meaning:

Red: Stop - Come to a complete stop and do not proceed until the light turns green.

Yellow: Slow - Prepare to go. The light is about to turn green.

Green: Go - Proceed with caution. The road is clear, but be aware of pedestrians and other vehicles.

2/7

No Left Turn

Prohibits drivers from making a left turn at the intersection. Ensures safe and smooth traffic flow.

3/7

Hump ahead

This sign warns drivers of a speed bump or hump on the road ahead. It signals the need to slow down to avoid damage to the vehicle and ensure safety.

4/7

No Right Turn

This sign indicates that turning right at the intersection is prohibited. It helps manage traffic flow and prevent accidents in areas where right turns could be dangerous.

5/7

No Overtaking

Prohibits overtaking in the area for safety due to curves, hills, or other hazards.

6/7

No Parking

Indicates that parking is prohibited in the designated area to ensure smooth traffic flow and safety.

7/7

Speed Limit

Indicates the maximum speed allowed on this road segment. Helps maintain safe driving speeds.
